About Us

We have been a leader company in the moving business for more than 10 years. We give our clients an experience and reliable service based on the highest quality standards and international support.

Vision: To be the company of logistics, warehouse and transfers of national and international merchandise, leader in Costa Rica and with worldwide projection.

Mission: We are the logistics, warehousing and transportation company of national and international merchandise that seeks to give the best service of world quality in Costa Rica and the rest of the world.

Values:

Timeliness: Our client and his time is the most important thing for us, the effectiveness that we offer our clients is time.
Responsibility: El cumplimiento de las necesidades de nuestros clientes son el baluarte de nuestro crecimiento.
Commitment: To solve the requests of our clients so that their objectives are fulfilled always and we cultivate satisfied customers.
Respect: An important pillar in our company for the good harmony between our collaborators and clients.
Effective: The work that we do try to do it in the best and immediate way, complying with efficient and reliable processes for the quality of our services to the clients.
Teamwork: It is an important pillar for our company that the team work is strengthened and trained to provide a quality service.
